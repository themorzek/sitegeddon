About Spruce Forests

A spruce forest is a natural or artificially planted forest dominated by spruce (Picea). These forests are classified as evergreen, dark-coniferous forests.

Tree Species in Spruce Forests

Sequoia
Fir.
Pine.

Animals of Spruce Forests

Squirrel, elk.
Bear, wolf, lynx.
Hedgehog, marten.

A Bit About Owls

The owl is a predatory bird from the order "Strigiformes," which includes more than 200 species. Owls are found all over the world except Antarctica. Some features:
Most species are nocturnal.
Owls have large round eyes located in front, not on the sides.
The pupil narrows and widens during breathing and under different light.
Owls have very keen hearing.
Wings are rounded at the edges and curved toward the body.
Feet are almost entirely covered with feathers.
Almost all species have a muted coloration in gray-brown tones.
Owls mainly live a sedentary lifestyle and do not like long flights.
Their diet is primarily animal-based food.
